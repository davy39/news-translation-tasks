Svelte is a front-end JavaScript framework for making interactive webpages. Svelte has grown in popularity quite a bit over the last few years.

We just published a free 23-hour course on the freeCodeCamp.org YouTube channel that will teach you how to use Svelte.

Li Hau Tan developed this course. Li is a core maintainer of Svelte so he is the perfect person to teach this course.

Lee starts at the absolute beginning with creating your first Svelte component and continues on to teach every concept you need to know to develop applications using Svelte.
