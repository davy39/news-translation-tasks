Flask is a popular web development framework for Python.

We just published a course on the freeCodeCamp.org YouTube channel that will help you learn Flask by building your own e-commerce platform with its own robust authentication system.

The course was developed by Jim from JimShapedCoding. Jim has made many popular coding tutorials.

Here are the sections in this course:

* Styling & Templates
* Sending data to Templates
* Template Inheritance
* Models and Databases
* Project Restructure
* Model Relationships
* Flask Forms
* Flask Validations
* Flashes & Advanced Validations
* User Authentication
* Logout and Customizations
* Item Purchasing
* Item Selling

Watch the full course below and on the [freeCodeCamp.org YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qr4QMBUPxWo) (6 hours).
