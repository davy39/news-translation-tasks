The Daily Stand Up (aka DSU) is a part of the Agile software development process (and SCRUM). These meetings are a short, helpful, and necessary part of the day for many developers.

A lot of new developers wonder what web developers do on a day-to-day basis. In this video, Anissa Deanna shares all about the Daily Stand Up meeting, which is common in many development jobs. She also gives tips to help you through your first DSU.
