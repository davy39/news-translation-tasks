If you keep hearing acronyms like CORS, CSP, and SSL - but don't know what they mean - you're in luck. [This article by Austin Tackaberry](https://medium.freecodecamp.org/f90beaf4dd41) covers these security concepts in detail. It's a great primer for new developers.

Here are some of the concepts it explains in just 7 minutes:

* Cross-Origin Resource Sharing (CORS)
* Content Security Policy (CSP)
* HTTPS (HTTP Secure)
* HTTP Strict-Transport-Security (HSTS)
* Man in the Middle Attacks (MITM)
* ...and several others
