Almost every business has some sort of computer network. Lots of tech jobs now require some basic knowledge of how networks work.

We have released a free 9-hour course that will prepare you to configure, manage, and troubleshoot both wired and wireless computer networks.

Industry veteran and college instructor Brian Ferrill – who has held a variety of network and security certifications – teaches this course.

This course will teach you about:

* networking concepts
* network infrastructure
* network operations
* network security
* and network troubleshooting

It will also give you an understanding of enterprise technologies, including cloud and virtualization technologies.

And by the end of the course, you will even be ready to take the CompTIA Network+ exam. Passing this exam will give you an industry-recognized certification that verifies you have acquired essential knowledge and skills related to computer networking.
