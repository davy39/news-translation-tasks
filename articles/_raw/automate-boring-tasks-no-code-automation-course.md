If you are tired of doing monotonous business tasks, automation can make your life easier.

We just posted a no-code automation course on the freeCodeCamp.org YouTube channel that will help you increase your productivity while working less. Ania Kubów, one of freeCodeCamp's most popular instructors, developed this course. She'll guide you through the process of building smart, automated solutions, even if you're not a tech expert.

This course is designed for the doers, creators, makers. Basically, if you have the drive to construct smart solutions through automation, this course is for you.

Ania structured this course to take you on a step-by-step journey through seven different automation pipelines, ranging from the basics to more advanced levels. In 3 hours, you'll learn how to automate a variety of tasks using Make and other tools, without needing any coding expertise.

The course will guide you through creating efficient automation pipelines for tasks like lead generation from social media platforms, custom email dispatch from Google Sheets, and parsing JSON for developer tasks. You'll explore how to employ automation in e-commerce to generate detailed product descriptions and learn to integrate OpenAI ChatGPT for content creation.

Additionally, the course covers the creation of an event ticketing system using Google's suite and automating social media activities to enhance your digital presence. By the end of this course, you'll have the knowledge and skills to apply automation across various tasks, freeing up your time to focus on more significant challenges and opportunities.

Here is the course outline:

### Lead Management Automation

* Lead organization with Facebook Lead Ads and Google Sheets
* Lead Enrichment with web-hooks, HubSpot, Slack and Clearbit
* Lead classification with Typeform and Hubspot
* Qualify incoming leads with Google Sheets and Clearbit

### Ecommerce Automation

* Generate Product Descriptions with ChatGPT and Google Sheets

### Ticketing/Barcode Automation

* Generate barcoded tickets with Google Sheets and Google Docs

### Automation In-house

* Generate calendar events with Trello and Google Calendar
* Make a to-do list from Discord Messages and Google Sheets

### Automation and AI

* Automate emails with ChatGPT and Gmail

By understanding the thought process behind automation and learning to implement it in various business aspects, you'll unlock new levels of productivity and innovation.

Watch the full course on [the freeCodeCamp.org YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5kJv254sebQ) (3-hour watch).
