On this week's episode of the podcast, I interview Kamran Ahmed. He's a software engineer and founder of [Roadmap.sh](http://Roadmap.sh), which has skill tree roadmaps for lots of developer fields, such as DevOps. As a teacher, he's also a Google Developer Expert and a GitHub Star.

We talk about:

* Kamran's tips for finding the right open source projects to contribute to
    
* The story behind [Roadmap.sh](http://Roadmap.sh), his popular developer website
    
* Other specialized open source Kamran has built over the years
    
* How Kamran became a Google Developer Expert and GitHub Star
