In Gmail, you can set a profile picture. Everyone else who uses Gmail will see this photo when they receive an email from you.

When you set a profile picture, Google will use it in all of their products, including Google Meet, Google Docs.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/04/Re__Learn_how_to_code_video_games__free_7-hour_Unity_3D_course__-_quincy_freecodecamp_org_-_freeCodeCamp_org_Mail-1.png)

## How to Add a Gmail Profile Picture in Google

Instead of telling you to click through a bunch of links to find where you can set this, I'm just going to give you a link to it. **Here it is: [Google's My Account About Me page](https://myaccount.google.com/personal-info?gar=1).**

This is what the page looks like:

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/04/Google_Account.png)
_Google's About Me page. You can change a lot of other important things here as well, including important privacy settings._

Note that the process of adding a new profile picture and changing your existing picture is exactly the same. Here's how to do this.

First, click on the circle with the camera icon in it.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/04/Google_Account-2.png)
_Google Personal Info panel, with an editable profile picture button_

Then you can upload a new picture or choose one from your Google Images folder.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/04/Google_Account-3.png)
_Google's photo upload interface_

Once you've chosen an image, you can tweak it before confirming. The image has to be a perfect square, but Google gives you a tool to reposition or zoom in and out.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/04/Google_Account-1.png)
_Google's profile picture editing interface_

Presto. You have now updated your profile photo.

## How to Remove your Profile Photo from Google

You can't technically remove your profile photo â€“ only update it.

But here is a 400 by 400 pixel white square that you can right click and save to your hard drive.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/04/white-square-for-profile-image.jpg)
_You can right click this square. (It's white and you may not be able to see it, but it is there.) Save it to your hard drive, then upload this to Google to replace your unwanted profile image._

Then you can go to [Google's My Account page](https://myaccount.google.com/personal-info?gar=1) and upload the image to serve as your new image.

Now your profile image appear completely blank.
