"Design is a skill, and like any other skill, it can be learned." That is the key message Patrik Krupar wants you to take away from his [freeCodeCamp article](https://medium.freecodecamp.org/1f64ce28c38d).

Patrick wasn't always good at design. Games like Minecraft inspired him to dig in deeper and learn how to make his web apps more beautiful and easier for users to understand.

In his article, he walks you through how to adjust your mindset, learn the most popular design tools, and start building layouts.

Read Patrik's full article [on freeCodeCamp's Medium publication](https://medium.freecodecamp.org/1f64ce28c38d) (10 minute read).
