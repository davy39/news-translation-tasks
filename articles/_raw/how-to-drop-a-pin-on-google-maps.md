If you want to save a new location you discovered on Google Maps, you need to drop a pin on that location. 

In this article, I will show you how to drop a pin in Google Maps from your phone or computer.

## How to Drop a Pin on Google Maps (Mac or Windows)

Step 1: Sign into your Google Account

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-6.31.17-AM.png)

Step 2: Click on the Google apps icon and go to Maps

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.41.51-AM.png)

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-6.32.11-AM.png)

Step 3: Search for a location using the search bar 

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-6.34.02-AM.png)

Step 4: Click on the pin icon next to the location you want to save

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-6.35.35-AM.png)

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.43.14-AM.png)

You should see a red pin icon on the location in the map.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-6.36.22-AM.png)

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.43.53-AM.png)

### How to drop a pin manually

Go to any location on the map and click on the icon next to the location you want to save. 

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-6.38.28-AM.png)

You should then see a red icon next to the location.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-6.38.56-AM.png)

### How to save the location

Once you pin a location, then click on save.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.54.40-AM.png)

Then you can choose which list you want to save your pinned location to.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.55.11-AM.png)



Then you should see it marked as saved. 

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.55.56-AM.png)

To find all of your saved locations, click on the menu icon and then click on Your Places. 

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.58.28-AM.png)

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.59.05-AM.png)

Then you can click on the list where the location was saved to.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-10.00.10-AM.png)

### How to share a location

Click on the Share icon under the pinned location.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-10.17.32-AM.png)

Then you can choose where to share your pinned location.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-10.18.25-AM.png)

### How to remove a pin

You can click on the `X` in the search bar.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.45.31-AM.png)

## How to Drop a Pin on Google Maps (phone)

If you haven't downloaded the Google Maps app on your phone you will need to do so. 

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/IMG_7EC548FE5DFA-1.jpeg)

Step 1: Open the application and search for a location

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/IMG_34F953BEA7EF-1.jpeg)

Step 2: Click on the location and you should see a red pin on the map

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.46.29-AM.png)

### How to drop a pin manually

On the map, go to your location of choice and press and hold your finger down on that location.

You should then see a red pin appear next to the location. 

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.47.44-AM.png)

To save your location, scroll through the options and click on Save.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/IMG_3551A21D19C8-1.jpeg)

Click on the list where you want to save it.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/IMG_974C885FBC14-1.jpeg)

### How to share your pinned location

You can find your pinned locations at the bottom of the app.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/IMG_1A73D60E475E-1.jpeg)

Then click on the share icon at the top of the screen.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/IMG_70D52DCF175D-1.jpeg)

Choose the method for sharing your pinned location.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/IMG_14A662267502-1.jpeg)

### How to remove a pin

To remove a pin, then you can click on the `x` in the search bar. 

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2021/10/Screen-Shot-2021-10-08-at-9.48.18-AM.png)
