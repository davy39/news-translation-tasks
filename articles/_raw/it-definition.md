IT (pronounced I.T.) stands for information technology.

IT is a broad term that refers to jobs that involve technology in business. Developers, network engineers, UI/UX designers, QA testers, and customer support are all IT jobs.

If the job involves computers, technology, and working with data, it's considered IT.
