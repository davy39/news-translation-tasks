Sometimes you might accidentally close your Word application without saving your file. You might think that your progress has been lost, but there's actually a way to recover the file without losing any of your content.

And that is exactly what this article will show you how to do.

To follow along with this tutorial, it'll be helpful if you have a Word document you can work with. Here is the one I'll be using:

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2022/01/word.png)

I have closed the application without saving the document. Now let's start the easy restoration process.

### **Step 1 – Create a blank document in Word**

* Launch the application again.
* On the startup page, click on "Blank document".

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2022/01/blank.png)

### **Step 2 – Use the Open option**

* Press **Ctrl+O**. 
* Alternatively, you can click on the **File** option in your blank document's header and then click **Open** on the sidebar.

### **Step 3 – Click on "Recover Unsaved Documents"**

* Click on the **Recover Unsaved Documents** which is usually at the bottom of the page.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2022/01/recover.png)

If you do not see the **Recover Unsaved Documents** button, then follow these steps to see the options:

1. In your blank document, press **Ctrl+O** or click on **File** which is at the header section.
2. Click on **Info** which you will find in the sidebar.
3. Click on **Manage Document**.
4. Click on **Recover Unsaved Document**.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2022/01/recover-unsaved-document.png)

### **Step 4 – Choose unsaved documents**

After you have clicked the **Recover Unsaved Documents** button, a new window should pop up with a list of unsaved documents. Go through the list and open your document and all your progress should still be intact.

![Image](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/content/images/2022/01/pop-up.png)

## Conclusion

In this article, we went over the steps required to recover an unsaved Word document. These steps can be used anytime you close Word without saving a document. 

Don't forget to save this time!
