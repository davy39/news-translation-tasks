Learn tips and strategies for mastering technical whiteboard coding interviews in this video from Sam Gavis-Hughson of byte-by-byte.com. You will learn how to solve many common interview problems and understand the concepts behind the solutions.

Coding interviews aren't all about how good you are at software engineering.  
They are about how well you play the coding interview game. This video will help you beat the game.

Here are some concepts and questions covered in this video:

* Median of Arrays
* Missing Two Numbers
* 0-1 Knapsack
* Integer to Roman Numeral
* Matrix Product
* Autocomplete
* Build Order
* Find All Duplicates
* Priority Queue
* List Cycles

You can watch the full video course on the [freeCodeCamp.org YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iAHQopLuZ4Q) (4.5 hour watch).
